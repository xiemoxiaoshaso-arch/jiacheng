### Product Features & Technical Advantages:

This **51.2V 100Ah Lithium Iron Phosphate Battery Pack** is tailored specifically for residential solar energy storage systems (Residential ESS) and micro commercial/industrial backup power. It utilizes a modular design that supports multi-unit parallel expansion.

1. **Intelligent Integrated BMS**: Equipped with a built-in high-precision Battery Management System (BMS) that monitors cell voltage, temperature, and current in real time, supporting multiple electrical protections against overcharge, overdischarge, overcurrent, short circuit, and extreme temperatures.
2. **Excellent Inverter Compatibility**: Supports native CAN/RS485 communication protocol integration with globally mainstream solar inverter brands (such as GoodWe, Growatt, Deye, Solis, etc.).
3. **Extended Service Life**: Adopts automotive-grade high-quality LFP cells, yielding a designed service life of 10 to 15 years, delivering safe and worry-free green household power security.
