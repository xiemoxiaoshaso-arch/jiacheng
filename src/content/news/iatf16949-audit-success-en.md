Recently, an expert auditor panel from an internationally recognized quality certification authority conducted a rigorous, multi-day annual surveillance audit of JC New Energy's (JC BATTERY WAY) IATF 16949:2016 Quality Management System. Following intensive field evaluations, JC New Energy successfully cleared the review with a flawless "Zero Non-Conformities" score.

### 1. Comprehensive On-Site Audit Applauds Production Consistency

During the audit window, the evaluation panel conducted exhaustive on-site inspections across JC New Energy's electrode coating cleanrooms, automated winding and assembly facilities, automated Pack assembly lines, the CNAS national-standard joint laboratory, and the centralized R&D center. The experts thoroughly scrutinized over ten core operational processes, including product design validation, raw material supply chain controls, MES digital production tracking, and non-conforming product protocols.

The audit panel concluded that JC New Energy has achieved elite industry benchmarks regarding manufacturing process automation, moisture/oxygen precision controls, and individual cell consistency checks, highly praising the company's long-term adherence to ultra-strict quality control parameters.

### 2. Automotive-Grade Rigor: Premium Manufacturing Transformed for Energy Storage

**IATF 16949** is globally recognized as the most stringent entry barrier for entering global automotive Tier-1 OEM supply chains. Successfully passing this annual surveillance audit proves that JC New Energy not only possesses stable, mature capabilities to serve passenger EVs and electric bus markets but also successfully applies the same automotive-grade manufacturing rigor and precision standards to assemble its Commercial & Industrial storage battery packs.

Moving forward, we will firmly uphold our core quality policy of "Safety First, Prevention as Priority, and Continuous Improvement," leveraging automotive-grade manufacturing standards to deliver premium, high-safety, and ultra-reliable power and energy storage battery portfolios to our global clientele.
