Recently, JC New Energy (JC BATTERY WAY) successfully completed full-process equipment commissioning and calibration for its Phase II Smart Energy Storage Cell Production Line at its smart manufacturing headquarters, marking its official entry into commercial production. This milestone signifies a major leap forward for JC New Energy in large-scale, high-volume manufacturing within the global energy storage sector.

### 1. Industry 4.0 & Class 10,000 Cleanroom Smart Workshop

The Phase II smart production line features the industry's most advanced Industry 4.0 fully automated manufacturing equipment, achieving a staggering overall automation rate of **98.5%**. The facility environment strictly complies with automotive-grade Class 10,000 dust-free cleanroom standards, maintaining a stable dew point below -40°C. This extreme environmental control minimizes micro-particle and humidity contamination during critical coating and winding processes.

"Battery consistency directly governs the overall lifespan and thermal safety of energy storage systems," stated the Director of JC New Energy's Quality Control Center. "Utilizing our end-to-end digital MES traceability platform, we execute 100% automated data capture across more than 100 critical control nodes—including slurry ratio, coating thickness, and laser welding energy. This ensures full-lifecycle, code-scannable traceability for every single cell leaving our factory."

### 2. 15 GWh Projected Capacity: Relieving Global Supply Shocks

With the successful commissioning of Phase II, JC New Energy's cumulative projected manufacturing capacity will officially climb to **15 GWh**. This major expansion will significantly relieve the current supply deficit for our high-capacity, storage-grade LFP cells across overseas markets, securing robust global deliveries and agile custom engineering setups for our Germany Service Center and North America Office.
